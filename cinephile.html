Cinephile

Movies aren’t just entertainment — they’re echoes of emotion, history, and human truth.

As a devoted cinephile, I explore cinema like a scholar studies scripture. From golden-age classics to silent frames soaked in symbolism, I watch with intent and wonder.

I write reflections, dissect character arcs, and lose myself in soundtracks that haunt and heal.

This space is for my film thoughts — the stories that stayed with me, and those I dream of creating someday.
