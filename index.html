About Me

I'm a student with a passion for words, visuals, and ideas. I serve as the Executive Secretary of the SPSC Public Speaking and Debating Club, where I also engage as a debater and designer. I'm a self-taught writer, researcher, and graphic designer with no formal degrees, just deep curiosity and relentless drive. I've created a variety of posters and logos across different themes and tones — from academic to artistic. I’m especially strong in creative writing, where imagination meets emotion. Cinema inspires me, debate sharpens me, and design lets me breathe color into thought. I may not be a professional, but I work with the mindset of one. My skills are still evolving, but my commitment is unwavering.
